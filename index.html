Stefaniia Bodnia is a designer originally from Ukraine, now based in The Hague, where she graduated from the Royal Academy of Art. Over the past few years, she has developed an independent practice at the intersection of art, design, and investigative research.

Narrative serves as her core medium, with a research-driven approach shaping her choice of form. Her work frequently manifests as visual poetry and essaying, realised through publications, installations, and visual communication. Stefaniia’s practice reflects a dynamic interplay of design elements, often focusing on projects with a sense of urgency and political or social relevance.
