Introduction

This research navigates the intersections of media materialism, ecological challenges, and geopolitical dynamics through an exploration of the resources embedded in contemporary technologies sourced from the South-Eastern region of Ukraine. The narrative unfolds through 3 stories touching upon the geological aspect of media and its material essence. This revelation is mirrored in the visual essay, where the absence of visibility and reachability is portrayed through obscurity of images originating from the region.
